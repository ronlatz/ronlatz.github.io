Whole Food Plant-Based: Resources


My family went from eating what we thought was a healthy American diet to an even healthier lifestyle when we all committed to follow a vegan diet consisting entirely of plant based foods. The food groups we stick to include vegetables, legumes, fruits, grains, nuts and seeds. We joke about the fact that we do not eat anything that has a mother, a head or poops. At first this may sound like a limited diet that is bland and unappealing, but that could not be farther from the truth.

My wife Julie has scoured the internet and a multitude of cookbooks (listed at the bottom of this page) and prepares incredible tasting dishes for us daily. Sometimes she modifies recipes because in addition to avoiding animal protein like red meat, fish, chicken, turkey, etc., we also avoid dairy and eggs. I whole-heartedly recommend that the decision to lead a whole food plant-based diet be done as a family. It makes it a lot easier when everyone is in the same mindset and the foods in your home are fully complementary to this lifestyle. Of course when outside the house you have to be steadfast in your decision to follow this diet when presented with animal product choices at work, restaurants, event functions and other peoples homes. But it is all doable. I have had some excellent vegan dishes (not even on the menu) at steakhouses when I call ahead and speak with the manager. The focus on the health benefits of eating plant-based is growing and the forces are at work in our government to making changes in the face of increased pressure from entrenched meat, dairy and egg industries protecting their profits.

I have become particularly interested in learning more about the health benefits of a plant based vegan diet and have read many books and watched videos on the subject. Julie and I even went on a 7 day vegan cruise where we attended breakout sessions given by medical doctors, research scientists, health coaches and chefs who have chosen to help their patients live in vibrant health by adapting this type of eating program.

The purpose of this website is to share with you the list of resources that I feel could help you in evaluating whether you and your family may want to take your diet and your health to the next level. I would be more than happy to speak to you if you have any questions or concerns so feel free to email me and we can arrange a time to speak.
